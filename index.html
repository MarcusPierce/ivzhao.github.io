About & Contact

I'm Ivan Zhao, currently in my mid-20s. In my university years I set my mind on pushing the frontiers of cognitive science, then realized that mostly meant busy applying for university grants. Now I have set my mind on the web.

I like design, especially design with clarity. It doesn't necessarily imply minimalism (as our human visual systems thrive in messy environments), instead I think the goal is to build a clear path between the screen and the views' perception - an information highway you may say. In order to do that I draw inspirations from the crafts of typography & cartography and the sciences of visual analytics.

I spend most of the days coding, thinking about modularity, lightweight-ness, and how to make my programs essay-like self-explanatory. I like Python, Javascript/Coffeescript, Google App Engine & Cocoa, also affluent with HTML/CSS, jQuery and PostScript.
